FAQs
How do I fund my wallet?
›
Go to Wallet → Fund Wallet, choose a payment method (card, bank transfer, or USSD), enter amount, and confirm.
Can I use Koborush in Ghana?
›
Yes! Koborush supports Nigeria, Ghana, Benin, and more. Select your country during registration.
How long does a transaction take?
›
Most transactions are instant. In rare cases, allow up to 5 minutes. Contact support if delayed beyond that.
