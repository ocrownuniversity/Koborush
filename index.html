FAQs
How do I fund my wallet?
›
Go to Wallet → Fund Wallet, select a method (card, bank transfer, USSD, or OPay), enter amount and confirm.
Who is ClubKonnect?
›
ClubKonnect is our VTU API provider for airtime, data, electricity, cable TV, and school PINs. They ensure instant delivery 24/7.
Can I use KOBOrush in Ghana?
›
Yes! KOBOrush supports multiple African countries. Select your country during registration to see local services.
